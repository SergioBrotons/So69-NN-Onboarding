## Section 3 — Core Explanation

### Concept Definition

KPI governance requires: single source of truth for definitions, clear formulas and parameters, documentation of calculation methodology, version control for changes, and change management process. Ungoverned KPIs lead to conflicting reports.

---

## Section 4 — Executive Scenario

**Scenario**: Marketing and Operations report different conversion rates. Governance: establishing single definition, documented formula, and approval process for changes.

---

## Section 5 — Failure Patterns

1. Multiple definitions
2. Missing documentation
3. Uncontrolled changes
