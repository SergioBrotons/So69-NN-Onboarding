## Section 3 — Core Explanation

### Concept Definition

Legal objections require: understanding the specific concern (GDPR, liability, data processing agreement), providing appropriate documentation (DPAs, compliance certifications), engaging legal teams appropriately, and knowing when to escalate.

---

## Section 4 — Executive Scenario

**Scenario**: Customer legal demands GDPR-compliant DPA with specific clauses. Response: provide standard DPA, escalate unusual requests to legal team, maintain deal momentum.

---

## Section 5 — Failure Patterns

1. Making commitments beyond authority
2. Dismissing legal concerns
3. Delaying unnecessarily
