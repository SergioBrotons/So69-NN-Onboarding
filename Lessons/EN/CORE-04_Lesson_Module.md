## Section 1 — Strategic Purpose

CORE-04 exists to ensure personnel can deliver executive communications that maintain institutional discipline. Executive pitches represent high-stakes communications where disciplined narrative directly impacts strategic outcomes.

---

## Section 2 — Foundational Linkage

Builds upon CORE-01 (Brain + Muscle) and CORE-02 (Offline Business Intelligence) to ensure pitch content aligns with institutional framework.

---

## Section 3 — Core Explanation

### Concept Definition

A disciplined executive pitch is a structured communication that conveys institutional value propositions with precision, avoiding exaggeration while maintaining persuasive impact. It requires balancing compelling narrative with factual accuracy.

---

## Section 4 — Executive Scenario

**Scenario**: Presenting quarterly results to executive leadership with clear ROI demonstration while maintaining realistic projections.

---

## Section 5 — Failure Patterns

1. Exaggerating capabilities
2. Overpromising results
3. Using marketing language instead of disciplined narrative
4. Missing the "Why Now" component

---

## Section 6 — Practical Exercise

**Task**: Deliver a 5-minute executive pitch on a project, recorded and self-assessed using the discipline rubric.
