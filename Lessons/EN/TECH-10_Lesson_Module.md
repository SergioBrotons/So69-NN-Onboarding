## Section 3 — Core Explanation

### Concept Definition

Retention management includes: policy definition (what to keep how long), automated enforcement (deletion triggers), and compliance reporting. Access control includes: authentication, authorization, and audit logging. Technical implementation of governance policies.

---

## Section 4 — Executive Scenario

**Scenario**: GDPR audit requires proof of data deletion. System shows: 90-day retention enforced, deletion automated, audit trail complete. Compliance demonstrated through technical controls.

---

## Section 5 — Failure Patterns
