## Section 1 — Strategic Purpose

LANG-05 ensures personnel can convert exaggerated marketing language into disciplined institutional narrative. Marketing often overstates capabilities; disciplined narrative maintains accuracy while remaining persuasive.

## Section 3 — Core Explanation

### Concept Definition

Marketing language prioritizes impact through exaggeration—"world-class," "revolutionary," "unmatched." Disciplined narrative maintains persuasive power through precision—"demonstrated 23% improvement in controlled testing." Conversion requires identifying hyperbolic claims and translating them to evidence-based statements.
