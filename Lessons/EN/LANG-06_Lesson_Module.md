## Section 3 — Core Explanation

### Concept Definition

Manifesto tone reflects institutional confidence and precision. It avoids hedging ("we hope to"), casual language ("pretty good"), or excessive qualification. Instead it uses direct statements ("the system delivers") grounded in evidence.

---

## Section 4 — Executive Scenario

**Scenario**: Rewriting a status report from casual language to manifesto tone.
