## Section 3 — Core Explanation

### Concept Definition

Structured discovery follows a framework: business context (company, role, strategy), pain identification (current challenges), technical environment (existing systems), decision process (timeline, stakeholders), and qualification (budget, authority, need). Discipline ensures consistent information capture.
