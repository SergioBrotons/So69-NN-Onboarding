## Section 3 — Core Explanation

### Concept Definition

Privacy by Design embeds privacy protection into system architecture rather than treating it as an afterthought. Executive communication must translate technical implementation to risk reduction, compliance assurance, and customer trust value.

---

## Section 4 — Executive Scenario

**Scenario**: Presenting privacy architecture decisions to board members who need risk and value understanding, not technical details.
